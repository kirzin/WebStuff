Welcome to Catto

Your friendly Cat Adoption site
Did you know that most cats do not have a home due to no fault of their own? It is a common myth to think that all cats up for adoption in shelters and rescues are damaged in some way. But, nothing could be less true! Cat shelters and rescues are full of lovable, active and healthy adoptable cats just waiting for someone to take them home. A majority of cats are given up when their prior owner no longer afford the financial requirements to keep them, got divorced, had a death in the family or other unexpected change in their family situation, or didn't realize how much time & attention a cat adoption deserves and needs.
